About

The "Video Games Sales with Ratings" dataset available on Kaggle provides a comprehensive overview of the gaming industry, merging sales data with critical and user ratings. It encompasses various details such as game titles, release dates, platforms, sales figures across different regions, and ratings from both critics and users. This dataset serves as a valuable resource for analyzing trends in game popularity, platform preferences, and the relationship between sales and ratings. Researchers and enthusiasts can leverage it to explore factors influencing game success and to gain insights into the dynamics of the gaming market.

Dataset Columns

The dataset features essential attributes of video games and their market performance. It includes details such as game titles, platforms, release years, genres, publishers, and sales figures in North America, European Union, Japan, other and global, enabling comprehensive analysis of the industry's landscape.

Columns

Name

The name of the video game.

Platform

The platforms on which the game was released.

Year of Release

The year the game was released.

Genre

The genre of the game.

Publisher

The publisher of the game.

North America Sales

Sales figures in North America (in millions).

European Union Sales

Sales figures in the European Union (in millions).

Japan Sales

Sales figures in Japan (in millions).

Other Sales

Sales figures in other regions (in millions).

Global Sales

Total global sales (in millions).

Critic Score

The aggregated score given by critics.

Critic Count

The count of critics who reviewed the game.

User Score

The aggregated score given by users.

User Count

The count of users who reviewed the game.

Developer

The developer of the game.

Rating

The age rating assigned to the game.
